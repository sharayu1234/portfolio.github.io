Introduction

If someone had told me 10 years ago that I would be where I am today, I never would have believed them. The shy, reliant and underconfident girl full of self-doubt grew into a driven, courageous and responsible women living her dream life thousands of miles away from her home in the city of Boston.

Hello, I'm Sharayu, a data analyst with a passion for using data to drive business decisions and tell compelling stories. I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Data Analytics at Northeastern University, where I also work as a research assistant. My research project in collaboration with NNCO (National Nanotechnology Coordination Office) involves discovering trends in the NanoEHS field by analyzing research papers from the past decade. My work primarily focuses on data cleaning, natural language processing, and creating network graphs using Python. Additionally, I'm a teaching assistant for the course Computation and Visualization for Analytics.

I am a member of the Husky Speaking Club at Northeastern University, where I sharpen my public speaking skills and engage in interesting conversations. Besides, I have a strong passion for writing, which I pursue as a hobby in my free time. When I'm not delving into data, I indulge myself in reading non-fiction books and exploring different cafes around Boston. As I write this, I'm sitting at the cozy George Howell Cafe on a lovely Sunday morning.

On my Portfolio Page, you'll find a collection of my work, including case studies, data visualizations, and reports. I hope that my portfolio gives you a better understanding of my skills and experience in the field of data analytics. If you're interested in collaborating with me or have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio!

How I discovered my passion for Data?

Growing up, I always saw people working at 9 to 5 jobs in tech as uncool. I never thought I would enjoy tech. Yet, like most confused souls, I chose Engineering, Mechanical Engineering to be precise.
